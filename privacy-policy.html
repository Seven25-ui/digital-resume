Privacy Policy for Seven33 Academy

At Seven33, accessible from seven33.site, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Seven33 and how we use it.

  • Log Files: We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.
  • Cookies and Web Beacons: Like any other website, Seven33 uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited. The information is used to optimize the users' experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser type and/or other information.
  • Google AdSense: Google is one of the third-party vendors on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to our site and other sites on the internet.
