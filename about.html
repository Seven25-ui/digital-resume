The Architect's Mission

I am Gene Guardiana, a System Architect dedicated to pushing the boundaries of mobile computing. My journey began with a simple question: "Can a smartphone build an Operating System?"

This website serves as a living documentation of my research in Low-Level Engineering and Full-Stack Development, all conducted within the Termux environment. My goal is to provide high-quality, free education for aspiring developers who only have a mobile device as their primary tool.
